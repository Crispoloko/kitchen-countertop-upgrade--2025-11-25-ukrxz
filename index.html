About KitchenPro

Our Journey

Founded in 2008, KitchenPro emerged from a simple vision: to make professional kitchen design accessible to everyone. What started as a small team of passionate designers has grown into a trusted name in kitchen transformations, serving over 500 satisfied clients across the region. Our founders, both experienced architects and designers, recognized a gap in the market for affordable yet premium-quality kitchen upgrades. Today, we combine decades of industry expertise with cutting-edge design trends to deliver exceptional results that exceed expectations. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to quality, and a deep passion for transforming ordinary kitchens into extraordinary spaces.

Our Mission & Values

At KitchenPro, our mission is to empower homeowners to create kitchen spaces that reflect their lifestyle and enhance their quality of life. We believe that every home deserves a beautiful, functional kitchen—regardless of budget constraints. Our core values center on transparency, quality, and customer satisfaction. We are committed to providing expert guidance that educates and empowers our clients, sourcing only premium materials that stand the test of time, and delivering exceptional service at every touchpoint. We measure success not just by completed projects, but by the lasting relationships we build and the positive impact we create in our clients' homes and lives. Our vision is to be the most trusted kitchen upgrade partner, known for integrity, innovation, and transformative results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about kitchen countertop upgrades

The timeline for a kitchen countertop upgrade typically ranges from 2-4 weeks, depending on several factors. Initial consultation and design phase takes 3-5 days. Material selection and ordering usually takes 5-7 days, depending on availability. The actual installation process typically takes 1-3 days for standard countertops. Complex designs or custom materials may require additional time. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during your consultation, and we pride ourselves on respecting deadlines while maintaining exceptional quality. We also offer expedited options for clients with urgent timelines.

We offer a comprehensive selection of premium countertop materials to suit every style and budget. Our most popular options include: Quartz (engineered stone offering exceptional durability and low maintenance), Granite (natural stone with unique patterns and timeless appeal), Marble (elegant and luxurious but requires more maintenance), and Laminate (budget-friendly with good design variety). Each material has distinct advantages in terms of durability, maintenance requirements, aesthetics, and cost. During your consultation, our experts will discuss the pros and cons of each option based on your specific needs, kitchen usage patterns, and design preferences. We can also show you samples and discuss long-term value and ROI for each choice.

Yes, we understand that kitchen upgrades are significant investments, which is why we offer flexible financing options to make your project more affordable. We partner with trusted financial institutions to provide competitive financing plans with various terms and interest rates. Financing options include: 0% APR for qualified customers (12-24 months), traditional financing with competitive rates, and flexible payment plans tailored to your budget. Our team will work with you to find the best financing solution that fits your financial situation. We also provide detailed cost breakdowns so you understand exactly where your investment is going. Contact us for a personalized quote and financing options discussion.

We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties on both materials and installation. Our warranty coverage includes: Lifetime warranty on material defects (covers manufacturing flaws and structural issues), 10-year warranty on installation workmanship, and 5-year warranty on sealant and finishing work. We also offer optional extended protection plans for additional peace of mind. All warranties are fully transferable if you sell your home, which adds value to your property. Our warranty is backed by our reputation and commitment to customer satisfaction. We encourage you to review our warranty details during your consultation, and our team is happy to answer any questions about coverage and terms.
