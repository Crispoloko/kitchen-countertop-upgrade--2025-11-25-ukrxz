About Backup Countertop Services

Transforming kitchens, enhancing homes, enriching lives

Our Journey

Founded in 2019, Backup Services LLC emerged from a simple vision: to make professional kitchen design accessible to everyone. What started as a small team of passionate designers has grown into a trusted name in kitchen transformations, serving over 500 satisfied clients across the region. Our founders, both experienced architects and designers, recognized a gap in the market for affordable yet premium-quality kitchen upgrades. Today, we combine decades of industry expertise with cutting-edge design trends to deliver exceptional results that exceed expectations. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to quality, and a deep passion for transforming ordinary kitchens into extraordinary spaces.

Our Mission & Values

At Backup Services LLC, our mission is to empower homeowners to create kitchen spaces that reflect their lifestyle and enhance their quality of life. We believe that every home deserves a beautiful, functional kitchenregardless of budget constraints. Our core values center on transparency, quality, and customer satisfaction. We are committed to providing expert guidance that educates and empowers our clients, sourcing only premium materials that stand the test of time, and delivering exceptional service at every touchpoint. We measure success not just by completed projects, but by the lasting relationships we build and the positive impact we create in our clients' homes and lives. Our vision is to be the most trusted kitchen upgrade partner, known for integrity, innovation, and transformative results.
